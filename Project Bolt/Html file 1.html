Welcome to Pawfect - Our Dog Feeding Program!

Pawfect is a compassionate online platform dedicated to ensuring no dog goes hungry. It allows users to pledge and commit to feeding a dog on a chosen date, fostering a community-driven effort to provide food and care to dogs in need.
